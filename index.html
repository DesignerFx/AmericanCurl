Our Objectives

Our cattery goal is to produce healthy & eye catching American Curl kittens for sale with well rounded out going personalities. Our focus is improving the overall quality, as we actively participate in CFA cat showS. Our cattery proudly follows the TICA Code of Ethics. We work hard to maintain the American Curl Breed Standards, keep track of our bloodlines, treat our kitties with love and respect, and stand behind our health guarantees and all of our adult cats used in our Breeding Program have been genetically tested for PRA and PK Deficiency and received Negative/Negative as a result. We take great pride in the care and health.

Frequently Asked Questions

It’s hard not to fall in love with these Wonderful Adorable Loving Companions and we get asked a lot of questions which is why we try as much as possible to educate everyone who is interested.

Are American Curls good pets?

Yes. The American Curl cat is very lively and joyous and loves to explore. If you’re interested in a cat that is sociable and good with kids, this might be a great choice for you. They are also capable of getting along with other cats and with cat-friendly dogs in the home. For cat lovers who are interested in finding a fun, friendly and interactive cat for the home, the American Curl cat might be a fantastic choice!
