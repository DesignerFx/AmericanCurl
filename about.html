Welcome To Amandacurls

About Our Cattery

My name is Amanda Kelley "AMANDACURLS" is a small but quality-oriented cattery, for 17 years we have had the pleasure of being American Curl Cat owners and breeder. We have been showing and breeding Curls exclusively in CFA (Cat Fanciers’ Association) and proudly follow the CFA BREED STANDARD. I have been breeding and raising several types of animals for decades and an animal lover since I was a child. I immediately fell in love with the American Curl, and after learning more about the breed I decided to raise these beautiful intelligent animals.
We raise our American Curl kittens for sale in our home with our Four children and our Two friendly dogs. We are dedicated to the breed and are dedicated to producing only the most beautiful, healthiest and highest quality American Curl kittens possible. We work hard to maintain the American Curl Breed Standards, keep track of our bloodlines, treat our kitties with love and respect, and stand behind our health guarantees.

All our kittens and cats are loved and cared for in our home. All our cats are indoor cats, we do not expose them to wild animals. Their health and welfare are our top priority. We believe in a clean and healthy environment for our cats by keeping a very clean and tidy household. Our goal is to breed beautiful, loving, and well socialized cats. We spend quality time with all our cats and kittens to ensure our kittens are well socialized. They are well taught and handled daily starting at a young age.

Our Cattery Goals

Our cattery goal is to produce healthy & eye catching American Curl kittens for sale with well rounded out going personalities. Our focus is improving the overall quality, as we actively participate in CFA cat showS. Our cattery proudly follows the TICA Code of Ethics. We work hard to maintain the American Curl Breed Standards, keep track of our bloodlines, treat our kitties with love and respect, and stand behind our health guarantees and all of our adult cats used in our Breeding Program have been genetically tested for PRA and PK Deficiency and received Negative/Negative as a result. We take great pride in the care and health.

I will NEVER breed American Curl kittens for quantity, but instead for quality, health and personality in mind. I follow the TICA Breeders Code of Ethics. All my breeding cats are DNA tested and scanned for Hypertrophic Cardiomyopathy by a cardiologist who comes to my home with the necessary portable equipment. All my cats have passed the tests with flying colors. If you are looking for a loving, affectionate American Curl kitten that will bring a smile to your face every time you look into those adoring, melt-your-heart faces, then contact us now!
