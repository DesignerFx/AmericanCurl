DELIVERY / SHIPPING YOUR KITTEN

We get asked a lot of times: Is it possible to ship my Kitten? We will provide transportation for your kitten if needed, We handle all delivery arrangements and details. Shipping is available within the United States and Canada only. Our American curl kittens for sale are being delivered considering the least amount of stress possible so as to ensure peace of mind to the purchaser as our kittens are well taken care of by pet nannies, shipping a kitten is safe and comfortable, don’t get mad when you hear a kitten can be shipped to you, shipping pets is usually safe and every airlines taking live animals have procedures in place to be sure they arrive in good health.  Kittens will not leave our cattery prior to reaching 11 weeks of age, it is crucial for their health and development to not be homed prior to this age. We personally hand deliver all our kittens for sale to the new families. However, when unable to proceed due to schedules we have ability to use Pet Nanny services to deliver our kittens.
Shipping is an additional cost to buyer of $200.00-$500.00 depending on airport destination and flight availability. The sooner transport is booked the cheaper this fee is. This fee Covers cost of Airline Ticket and all Veterinary/Airline company certifications needed to fly Via in cabin flight as we DO NOT ship via Cargo for ANY REASON.


Preparing for your American Curl Kitten

A designated small and comfortable room or space should be selected where your kitten can spend at least the first one to two weeks, until they are comfortable to explore additional parts of your home. Basic necessities such as food, water, and litter box are also needed. We always send your kitten home with a Quart-size bag of Royal Canin Kitten dry food, the same dry food that is fed at our cattery.
